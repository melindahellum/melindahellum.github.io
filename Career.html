Career

My career in the Food Service Industry started at a young age for me and I have been in it ever since. I have loved to cook and entertain since I was little and continue to do that in my career. I worked in a couple different restaurants in high school and following graduation attended Johnson & Wales University in Rhode Island. From there I opened and ran my own restaurant for a few years with family. After the closure of that I moved around and worked at a couple different restaurants until I got the job as Head Chef at Waldo County General Hospital . It is there that I developed an interest in Nutrition and decided to go back to school, I hope to educate others in leading healthier lifestyles when I graduate from UMaine in December 2019.
